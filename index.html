About State Based Systems

State Based Systems (SBS) is an electronic system owned by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) and enhanced in partnership with state insurance departments.

Our goal for SBS is to enable your state insurance department to more efficiently and effectively process license applications, renewals, inquiries, complaints, enforcement actions, etc. with a minimum of effort and remain compliant with national uniformity initiatives.
